Discovering new ways of providing healthy food to residents is a daily activity within Kamaliza. The low cost of energy needed to grow healthy and fresh food is in sharp contrast to the rising cost of grocery shopping. Community gardens in urban areas have proven to foster strong connections between people, thereby strengthening the whole community. Being involved in local agricultural projects and learning innovative ways to sustain themselves is a valuable opportunity for the youth involved. Kamaliza provides space for residents to start their own gardens and tend animals for a sustainable diet. These efforts not only bring financial rewards to the individuals involved but also provide the community with the benefits of a nutritious and healthy diet. Kamaliza is always searching for innovations and new ideas from others in order to increase production.

Kamaliza is always looking to make new connections and looking for opportunities that will be of mutual benefit. Our members are always available to communicate with those who are interested and will enthusiastically provide information about our projects and how to arrange a visit.
