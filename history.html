Kamaliza Environmental Youth Group was formed in 1996 to tackle the problem of waste lying stagnant in the community. Now almost twenty years on, Kamaliza is a landmark for environmental conservation and youth entrepreneurship. The group of over thirty young people provides opportunities for local youth who are hoping to make a difference, get involved or looking for guidance and mentorship.

Kamaliza has three main areas of focus: waste management, agriculture and sports. As a result of these efforts, the group has provided the community with countless opportunities through local and international partnerships.
